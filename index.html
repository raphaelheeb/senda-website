Cycling Passion Born in the Swiss Alps

Road & Gravel bikes. Built to outperform. Designed to stand out.

SENDA is a new Swiss cycling brand shaped by decades of riding, engineering and design. We build bikes that combine uncompromising performance with calm, modern aesthetics — machines you fall in love with when you ride them and when you look at them.

Born in the Alps, built for riders everywhere.

Our Origin

SENDA means path in Romansh — a reminder of where we come from and what drives us. Our roots lie in Switzerland’s landscapes, where precision, clarity and purposeful design are part of everyday life.

With backgrounds in materials science, architecture and high‑performance cycling, we create bikes that unite the best of both worlds: engineered for speed, responsiveness and control, designed with a clean, timeless aesthetic.

From Riders for Riders

SENDA is built with a community mindset — riders who value craftsmanship, innovation and the joy of discovering new roads together. If you want to be part of the first chapter, now is the moment.
